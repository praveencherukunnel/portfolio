About Me

I believe that a design vision is only as strong as its execution. As a UI/UX Designer with a deep understanding of frontend development, I occupy the vital space between how a product looks and how it actually lives in the browser.
My process begins with Human-Centered Design. I dive into user behavior to uncover the "why" behind their pain points, translating those insights into strategic wireframes and high-fidelity interfaces. But I don’t stop at the handoff. I leverage my technical background to ensure that every pixel, transition, and interaction is implemented with clean, accessible, and performant code.
By speaking both the languages of design and development, I eliminate the friction that often exists between the two. The result? Exceptional digital products that are as robust under the hood as they are beautiful on the surface.
